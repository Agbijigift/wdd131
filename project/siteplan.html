Site Name: Constructaora

The name is a creative blend of the word “Construct”, representing the building and construction industry, and a stylized ending “‑aora”, which gives it a modern, brandable feel

Site Logo:

site Purpose

Constructaora will be a web-based platform designed to connect individuals in need of building and property-related services with verified professionals. It will serve as a central hub where users can easily find and hire building constructors, civil engineers, building material suppliers, and land/property agents.

    3. “I’m a civil engineer in Port Harcourt. How can I list my services so clients can find me?”

    • Supports “List Your Services” page, registration, and dashboard.
